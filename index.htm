The Legend of Ascii

 

This game represents a reimagined iteration of a game I had previously developed. It takes the form of a multiplayer "batch" game, reminiscent of various online games from the 2000s like Club Penguin and others. The title itself draws inspiration from the renowned Legend of Zelda.

 

 

Within this game, your objective is to explore a vast map and discover various cosmetics and other items. While I am considering incorporating combat mechanics and additional features, if you have any specific requests, please feel free to reach out to me via email, and I will try to accommodate them.
